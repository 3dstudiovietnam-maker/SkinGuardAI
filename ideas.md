# DermIQ - AI Skin Health Monitor - Design Brainstorm

## Selected Approach: Clinical Serenity (Scandinavian Minimalism meets Medical UI)

**Core Principles**: Trust through cleanliness, calming whitespace, precision typography, soft organic shapes
**Color Philosophy**: Soft sage greens (#E8F0E8, #4A7C59) paired with warm whites (#FAFAF8) and charcoal (#2D2D2D). Green evokes health, nature, and trust. Accents of soft coral (#E8A598) for warnings.
**Layout Paradigm**: Card-based modular layout with generous padding. Bottom tab bar on mobile.
**Signature Elements**: Soft rounded cards with subtle shadows, organic blob shapes, thin line illustrations
**Typography System**: DM Sans (headings) + Inter (body)
